Taj Mahal

Agra, Uttar Pradesh

UNESCO Heritage Iconic Romance

🗓️ Best Time to Visit

October to March

⏱️ Duration

1-2 days recommended

🎫 Entry Fee

₹50 (Indians), ₹1100 (Foreigners)

About Taj Mahal

An eternal marble symphony of love, glowing golden at sunrise and silver under moonlight. The Taj Mahal stands as one of the most recognizable structures in the world and is considered one of the finest examples of Mughal architecture.

History

Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, the Taj Mahal stands as an eternal symbol of love. Construction began in 1632 and took 22 years to complete, employing over 20,000 artisans. The monument combines elements from Islamic, Persian, Ottoman Turkish, and Indian architectural styles.

Key Highlights

🕌

Main Mausoleum

Intricate marble inlay work with precious and semi-precious stones forming beautiful floral patterns.

🌳

Charbagh Garden

Mughal garden with reflecting pools that create stunning mirror images of the monument.

🕌

Red Sandstone Mosque

Beautiful mosque and guest house flanking the main mausoleum, built in red sandstone.

🌅

Mehtab Bagh

Moonlight Garden across the Yamuna River offering spectacular sunset views of the Taj.

Travel Tips

  • ✅
    Visit at sunrise for the best light and fewer crowds. The monument glows golden in the morning light.
  • 🎫
    Book tickets online in advance to avoid long queues at the entrance.
  • 📸
    Photography restrictions - Photography is not allowed inside the main chamber.
  • 👟
    Wear comfortable shoes for walking. You'll need to remove shoes before entering the main platform.
  • 🌙
    Full moon viewing - Special night viewing is available on full moon nights (book well in advance).

How to Reach

✈️ By Air

Agra has its own airport (Kheria Airport) with limited flights. Delhi's Indira Gandhi International Airport (230 km) is the nearest major airport.

🚂 By Train

Agra is well-connected by train. Gatimaan Express from Delhi is the fastest option (100 minutes). Agra Cantt is the main railway station.

🚗 By Road

Agra is connected by the Yamuna Expressway from Delhi (3-4 hours drive). Regular bus services available from major cities.
